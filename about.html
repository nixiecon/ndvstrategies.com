The story

I spent a decade in corporate operations, and I was always really good at it. What I didn't know was that I'm neurodivergent — and that I'd been forcing myself through life without the right support for 38 years.

As much as I loved running the operations at my last organization, I kept feeling worse and more useless every day. I couldn't figure out why. It wasn't until later that I learned what I'd been experiencing was autistic burnout. A lifetime of pushing through had finally caught up with me. I couldn't function. I had to stop.

Rebuilding from that is still in progress. But one thing got clear early: I wanted to keep the part of the work I love — fixing broken systems — without everything that was breaking me. That's what NDV Strategies is to me.
