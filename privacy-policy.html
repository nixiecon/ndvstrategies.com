Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026

NDV Strategies LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates ndvstrategies.com. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you submit our contact form: your name, email address, organization name, and the details of your inquiry. We do not collect payment information through this website.

We also collect usage data automatically through Google Analytics, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and determine whether we are a good fit to work together. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent. Usage data from Google Analytics is used to understand how visitors interact with the site so we can improve it.

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services that may collect or process data on our behalf:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy

  • GitHub Pages — website hosting. GitHub Privacy Statement

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to and fulfill your inquiry. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at contact@ndvstrategies.com.

Cookies

This site uses cookies through Google Analytics to track aggregate usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
