Engagement

How it works.

Pricing is inquiry-based. Each engagement is scoped to the problem — not to a package.

  1. 01

    You describe the problem.

    Email, call, or intake form. Specifics help — the spreadsheet that's breaking, the report that takes four hours every Monday.

  2. 02

    I scope the fix.

    You'll get a clear proposal — scope, timeline, deliverables, price. Nothing padded, nothing vague.

  3. 03

    I build it.

    Minimal management required on your end. I come back with progress, not asks.

  4. 04

    It runs without us.

    You get documentation, ownership, and a system that keeps working after I'm gone.
