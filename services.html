Engagement

How it works.

Pricing is inquiry-based. Each engagement is scoped to the problem, not to a package. And built to be adopted, not admired. It has to work on your worst day, not just in the demo.

  1. 01

    You describe the problem.

    Email or intake form. Specifics help, the spreadsheet that's breaking, the report that takes four hours every Monday.

  2. 02

    We scope the fix.

    You'll get a clear proposal, scope, timeline, deliverables, price. Nothing padded, nothing vague.

  3. 03

    We build it.

    Minimal management required on your end. We come back with progress, not asks.

  4. 04

    It runs without us.

    You get documentation, ownership, and a system that keeps working after we're gone.

Security & Trust

Your data, handled with care.

When you hire us, you hand over access to sensitive systems, bank records, client information, the inner workings of your organization. Here’s exactly how we protect it. Not promises in the abstract: specific habits, credentials, and systems.

  • Former Facilities Security Officer (FSO) for a Department of Defense (DoD) subcontractor, where I managed security and compliance.
  • Held a DoD Secret clearance in that role.
  • B.S. Computer Science and an MBA: I understand the systems I secure, not just the checklists.
  • 30+ years working with computers, since the DOS era.

  • Complete annual cybersecurity awareness training: PII/PCI handling, phishing, and social-engineering defense.
  • Working knowledge of NIST and CMMC compliance frameworks.
  • Hands-on experience handling PII and PCI-regulated data correctly.

  • NDV Strategies carries professional liability / Errors & Omissions (E&O) insurance.
  • So if something ever goes wrong, you’re protected, not left holding the risk.

  • Local-first. Wherever possible, your financial data is processed on our own systems, not uploaded to third-party tools or AI services.
  • Read-only by default. We request only the access a task actually needs.
  • Credentials are never hardcoded: they live in encrypted local config, never pasted into documents, code, or email.
  • Deleted within 30 days of completing the related work, never stockpiled.
  • Multi-factor authentication on every account that touches your information.

  • NDA-ready: happy to sign yours, or provide one.
  • Access is limited to what the project requires, and handed back when it’s done.
  • No sensitive documents over unsecured email.

  • My Statement Manager downloads bank statements and processes them entirely on a local machine.
  • Nothing is uploaded to an outside service to do the work.
  • It runs on the same deletion schedule above. Proof, not just promises.
